Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, then baked until bubbly and golden. It’s hearty, comforting, and perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredents

  • Lasagna noodles (pasta sheets)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  4. Add ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet and cook until browned; drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and herbs; simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with eggs, salt, pepper, and fresh basil.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella; repeat layers.
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
